<div align="center">
  <h1>🧰 05 - Fruiting Tub Assembly</h1>
  <i>Phase 2: Hardware Assembly</i>
</div>

---

> [!CAUTION]
> **Required Tool:** Step Bit. Using a standard spiral drill bit will catch and shatter the brittle plastic of the Sterilite tub.

**Tub Spec:** Sterilite 27 Qt Clear Plastic Latching Box
**Total Holes:** 10

### 📏 Long Sides (4 Holes Total)
1. Measure **5 inches up** from the bottom.
2. Measure **4.5 inches in** from the left corner. Drill a hole.
3. Measure **4.5 inches in** from the right corner. Drill a hole.
4. Repeat this exact process on the opposite long side.

### 📏 Short Sides (6 Holes Total)
**Bottom Holes:**
1. Find the exact middle of the wall.
2. Measure **5 inches up** from the bottom. Drill 1 hole.
3. Repeat on the opposite short side.

**Top Holes:**
1. Measure **1 inch down** from the middle of the handle latch. Make a mark with a sharpie.
2. Measure **1 inch to the left** of that mark and drill a hole.
3. Measure **1 inch to the right** of that mark and drill a hole.
4. *(You should now have 2 holes that are exactly 2 inches apart marked near the top handle).*
5. Repeat this process on the opposite short side.
