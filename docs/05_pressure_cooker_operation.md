# 05 - Pressure Cooker Operation

**Warning:** Running out of water will permanently warp the pressure cooker. Always ensure correct heat balance.

## Setup
1.  Put **8 cups of water** in the PC.
2.  Make sure everything on your lid is tight (no loose screws or loose safety valve).
3.  Ensure the rubber gasket is installed.
4.  Place the lid on the pressure cooker and turn the heat on high.

## Venting Protocol
1.  As it heats, the safety valve will pop upwards, locking the lid.
2.  Once popped, let steam blow out the valve for **10 minutes** at 5 to 8 psi. Do not have the heat so high that it builds too much pressure during venting.
3.  Place the 15-psi weight on the nipple.

## Achieving Heat Balance
1.  When the weight starts to wobble, turn the heat to minimum.
2.  Keep turning it down until it stops wobbling. If the weight stops moving completely, turn the heat up slightly and wait 5 minutes for the cooker to adjust.
3.  Find the transition zone: The weight should have a small wobble every second, or just enough pressure to start pushing up on the weight and pushing air out. 
4.  **Crucial:** If the weight wobbles too fast, you are building too much pressure and losing water quickly. Take your time finding the correct temperature on your burner and give it 5 minutes to adjust after every change.
