> [!TIP]
> **The Strategy:** This utilizes the classic "Bucket Tek" method, specifically adapted for loose fill coco coir (which is already fluffy out of the bag and requires no breaking).

<br>

## **🔥 Boiling**

1. Add **8 qts** of dry, loose coco coir directly to the 5-gallon bucket.
2. Fill your pressure cooker halfway with tap water. Place on a burner set to **High**.
3. Place the PC lid on top but **do not lock it**. Let the water come to a violent, strong roaring boil.
4. Turn the burner **Off**.

<br>

## **💧 Hydration & Pasteurization**

1. Quickly scoop out **16 cups** of the boiling hot water and pour it evenly over the coco coir in the bucket.
2. Snap the heavy-duty lid onto the bucket securely.
3. Place the entire 5-gal bucket directly into the remaining hot water left inside the pressure cooker pot. 
   *(This massive thermal blanket allows the bucket to hold pasteurization temps for a significantly longer period of time, guaranteeing flawless results).*

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Let it sit to pasteurize for **at least 12 hours**. Waiting **24 hours** is highly recommended to ensure the core of the coco coir fully cools to room temperature before you add live mycelium to it.
