# 10 - Grain Spawn Preparation

*Recipe yields exactly 6 Quart jars.*

## Hydrating the Popcorn
1.  Fill the 16 qt pressure cooker with 20 cups of water. Heat on high and bring to a boil.
2.  Add **1/2 cup of corn syrup** (use hot PC water to help dissolve it).
3.  Add **4 lbs of yellow popcorn**.
4.  Seal the PC lid. Vent for 10 minutes at 5-8 psi, place weight.
5.  Pressure cook on low for **15 minutes @ 15 psi**.
6.  Turn heat off and let depressurize for 45 minutes.

## Drying
1.  Pour corn into a strainer and let sit for 1 to 2 hours. **NO RINSING.** Let the heat and steam dry the kernels.
2.  Every 30 minutes, toss the popcorn from the strainer to a bowl and back to evenly mix them.
3.  The outside of the grain must be dry. You should have no water on your hand when you mix it up. (Expect 90 minutes and 3 tosses).

## Sterilizing the Jars
1.  Jar up the popcorn. Close your assembled grain lids fully and tight (no foil needed).
2.  Place jars in the PC raised off the bottom with a trivet (use 1/2 inch bolts under the trivet for extra room).
3.  Add 16 cups of water to the PC.
4.  Vent for 10 minutes. Pressure cook for **2 hours @ 15 psi**.
5.  Cool overnight with the PC lid on. 

## Post-Cook Conditioning
1.  Once cool, shake jars every 6 to 12 hours for 24-48 hours to redistribute remaining moisture. 
2.  **Safety Rule:** Do not hot shake. Popcorn shakes fine 12 hours after cooking.
3.  Let jars sit for a week before use to verify proper sterilization (ensure nothing unwanted is growing).
