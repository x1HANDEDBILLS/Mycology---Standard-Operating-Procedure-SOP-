# 01 - Hardware and Tools

These are your permanent, one-time investments required for the laboratory and fruiting environment.

## Main Equipment
* **Pressure Cooker:** Presto 16-Quart or 23-Quart (~90$ - 125$)
* **Still Air Box (SAB):** Pre-built or DIY Sterilite tub (~35$ - 40$)
* **Dehydrator:** Any standard food dehydrator (~250$ for commercial, cheaper options available)
* **Cabinet Shelf Organizer:** Wire rack to raise work area inside SAB (~10$)
* **Stir Plate:** Magnetic stirrer (~25$ - 40$)
* **Scale:** Gram scale (~18$)
* **Torch:** Propane torch (e.g., Coleman 16oz) to sterilize tools (~10$)

## Containers & Jars
* **Wide Mouth Mason Jars:** Quart / 32 oz (Box of 12) (~16$)
* **Media Bottle:** 500ml for agar (~11$)
* **Tub:** Sterilite 27 Qt Clear Plastic Latching Box (~8$)
* **Bucket:** 5-Gallon bucket with a good sealing lid (~10$)

## Tools & Hardware
* **Strainers:** Large multi-use colander (~10$ - Buy 2)
* **Spray Bottle:** Fine mist spray bottle for water (~15$)
* **Spray Bottle:** Standard spray bottle for 70% alcohol (~5$)
* **Drill Bits:** Step bit recommended, or 1/4-inch and 7/16-inch standard bits (~2$ - 7$)
* **Scalpel Handle:** (~7$)
* **Tweezers:** (~5$)
