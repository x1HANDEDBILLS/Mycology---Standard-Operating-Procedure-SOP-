# Still Air Box (SAB) Workflow

## Preparation
1.  Place all items (jars, agar, scalpel, rack) inside the SAB.
2.  Spray inside walls, floor, roof, and air with 70% alcohol. Spray jars and cups.
3.  Wipe the roof and viewing area with a paper towel. **Do not wipe the walls and floor**—leave them wet to catch floating particles.
4.  Let the SAB sit for 30 minutes before working.

## Movement Rules
* **Keep Elevated:** Keep arms and hands elevated and away from the wet floor.
* **No Hovering:** Never pass hands or tools over open containers. Contaminants can fall off your hands.
* **Tool Handling:** Rest tools on a raised block. Only touch tools by the upper handle. Only touch agar with the tip of the sterile blade.
* **Speed:** Move slowly and fluidly to prevent air currents. Minimize the time any container is open.
