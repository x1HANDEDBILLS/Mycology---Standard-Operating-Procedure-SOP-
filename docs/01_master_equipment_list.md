# Master Equipment List

## Main Items
* **Pressure Cooker:** Presto 16-Quart or 23-Quart (~90$ - 125$)
* **Still Air Box (SAB):** Pre-built or DIY Sterilite tub (~35$ - 40$)
* **Dehydrator:** Any standard food dehydrator (~250$ for commercial, cheaper options available)
* **Cabinet Shelf Organizer:** Wire rack to raise work area inside SAB (~10$)

## Jar Items
* **Wide Mouth Mason Jars:** Quart / 32 oz (Box of 12) (~16$)
* **Lids & Filter Patches:** Adherable 0.3 micron filters (~10$ - 20$)
* **Injection Ports:** Optional, for LC or spores (~7$)
* **Drill Bits:** Step bit recommended, or 1/4-inch and 7/16-inch bits (~2$ - 7$)

## Grain & Agar Items
* **Popcorn:** Yellow popping corn, 4 lbs (~5$)
* **Corn Syrup:** Light corn syrup with real vanilla (~3$)
* **Strainers:** Large multi-use colander (~10$)
* **Media Bottle:** 500ml (~11$)
* **Agar & LME:** Agar powder and Extra Light Malt Extract (~24$)
* **Portion Cups:** 3.25oz for agar pours (~8$)
* **Stir Plate:** Magnetic stirrer (~25$ - 40$)

## Equipment Items
* **Sterilization:** 70% Isopropyl Alcohol & Spray Bottle (~8$)
* **Tools:** Scalpel, Tweezers, Gloves (~24$)
* **Scale:** Gram scale (~18$)
* **Torch:** Propane torch for sterilizing tools (~10$)

## Fruiting Items
* **Coco Coir:** Loose fill bag preferred (e.g., FloraFlex) (~25$)
* **Tub:** Sterilite 27 Qt Clear Plastic Latching Box (~8$)
* **Bucket:** 5-Gallon bucket with a sealing lid (~10$)
* **Spray Bottle:** Fine mist spray bottle (~15$)
