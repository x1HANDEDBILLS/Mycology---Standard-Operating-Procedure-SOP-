# Fruiting & Tub Setup

## Substrate Pasteurization (Bucket Tek)
1.  Add 8 qts of dry, loose coco coir to a 5-gal bucket.
2.  Bring water to a roaring boil in the PC. Turn off the burner.
3.  Quickly scoop 16 cups of hot water onto the coco.
4.  Seal bucket lid securely. Place bucket into the remaining hot water in the PC to hold temp. 
5.  Let pasteurize for 12 to 24 hours to fully cool.

## Spawning to Bulk
1.  Wipe inside of the 27qt tub and lid with alcohol. Let dry.
2.  Dump 2 fully colonized grain jars into the tub (approx. 1:3 ratio).
3.  Squeeze handfuls of coco coir lightly until water just drips (like a kitchen sponge). Avoid over-squeezing or leaving streams of water.
4.  Mix 3-4 inches of hydrated coco evenly with the grain in the tub.
5.  Add a thin top layer of coco just to cover visible grains.
6.  Mist surface for 20 seconds.

## Fruiting Conditions
* **Environment:** Place in a darkish area (closets work well). A small LED can guide growth. 
* **Temperature:** Maintain 70F to 80F (72-74F is ideal). Ensure no fans blow directly on the tub.
* **Maintenance:** Open lid only to mist the tub twice daily. Do not modify air exchange; the tub is pre-drilled for fruiting. Expect full colonization and pins in about 2 weeks.
