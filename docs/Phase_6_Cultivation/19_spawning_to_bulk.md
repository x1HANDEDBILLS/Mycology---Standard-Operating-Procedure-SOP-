> [!IMPORTANT]
> **PPE Recommended:** Face mask and alcohol-sterilized gloves. Taking a shower and wearing clean clothes prior to this step is highly recommended.

### 🧽 Achieving Field Capacity (The Sponge Test)
1. Open the 5-gal bucket of pasteurized coco coir. Grab a handful at a time.
2. Slowly squeeze the coco in your hand to wring out excess water.
3. **Target:** Water should just be dripping out in heavy drops.
    * *Too Wet:* A continuous, unbroken stream of water pours out.
    * *Too Dry:* Very little water comes out, or requires maximum strength to get drops.
4. Aim for the moisture profile of a wrung-out kitchen sponge after a quick squeeze.

### 🧱 Tub Mixing
1. Wipe out the inside of the 27qt tub and lid with alcohol. Let sit for a minute and dry.
2. Dump **2 fully colonized grain jars** into the tub (Approx. a 1:3 ratio of grain to coco).
3. Add squeezed coco handfuls into the tub until it fills the bottom 3 to 4 inches thick. Leave some coco in the bucket for the final top layer.
4. Mix the coco and grain thoroughly with your hands. Ensure it is leveled out completely flat and even in the tub.
5. Add a thin casing layer of coco across the top—just enough to cover all visible exposed grains.
6. Spray the surface with a fine mist of water for about 20 seconds. 
7. Snap the lid onto the tub.
