> [!NOTE]
> Unlike older teks that require colonization periods, this tub design is introduced immediately into fruiting conditions upon creation.

### 🌡️ Environment
* **Location:** A darkish area (like a closet) is perfect. 
* **Lighting:** Mushrooms do not photosynthesize and do not *need* light to grow, but a small LED placed above them gives them a gravitational direction to grow toward.
* **Temperature:** Keep between **70°F to 80°F** (72°F to 74°F is ideal).
* **Airflow:** Ensure no fans or air currents are directly blowing on the tubs, as this causes the substrate to dry out much faster than the mycelium can handle.

### 💧 Maintenance
1. Contamination risk is exceptionally low at this stage because the grain is fully colonized and the coco coir has zero nutrients, but do not open the tub more than necessary.
2. Only open the lid to lightly mist the surface of the coco **twice a day**. 
3. No additional fanning or air exchange is needed; the strategically drilled 10 holes provide the correct passive balance of O2 and CO2.
4. Expect it to take 2 weeks to fully colonize the tub matrix. Keep misting 2 times daily until pins (baby mushrooms) form.
