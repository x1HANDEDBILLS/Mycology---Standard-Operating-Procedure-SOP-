# Grain & Agar Preparation

## Grain Prep (Popcorn)
1.  **Boil:** Fill 16 Qt PC with 20 cups of water. Bring to boil. Add 1/2 cup corn syrup and 4 lbs of yellow popcorn.
2.  **Cook:** Seal lid, wait for safety valve, vent for 10 mins. Add weight and cook for 15 minutes at 15 psi.
3.  **Depressurize:** Let depressurize for 45 minutes. 
4.  **Dry:** Pour into strainer. Let sit for 1-2 hours. Toss corn every 30 minutes to evenly dry the outside of the kernels. DO NOT RINSE.
5.  **Jar:** Load popcorn into jars. Close lids tightly (no foil needed). 
6.  **Sterilize:** PC jars on a trivet with 16 cups of water. Vent for 10 mins. Cook for 2 hours at 15 psi.
7.  **Cooling:** Cool overnight. Shake after 12 hours to redistribute moisture. Let sit for a week before inoculating to ensure sterility.

## Agar Prep
1.  **Mix:** Add 400ml water, 8g LME, and 8g agar to a jar. 
2.  **Dissolve:** Microwave for ~3 mins, stopping if it boils over. Repeat until dissolved. 
3.  **Bottle:** Pour into 500ml media bottle with a stir bar. Tighten lid.
4.  **Sterilize:** PC the bottle in a water bath (water 1 inch below agar level). Vent 10 mins. Cook for 20 minutes at 15 psi. Let depressurize for 45 mins.
5.  **Cool:** Stir on low until cooled to 120F before pouring.
