# 06 - PC Standard Operation

**Warning:** Running out of water will permanently warp the pressure cooker. Never bypass the venting protocol.

## 1. Setup
1.  Add the required amount of water for your specific recipe (usually between 8 to 16 cups, depending on the run length).
2.  Place your jars/bottles inside on a raised trivet.
3.  Ensure the rubber gasket is installed and lock the lid. Turn heat to high.

## 2. Venting Protocol
1.  Wait for the safety valve to pop upwards, locking the lid.
2.  Once popped, let steam blow out the vent pipe continuously for **10 minutes** to purge all internal air.
3.  Place the 15-psi weight on the nipple.

## 3. The Cook
1.  When the weight starts to wobble, turn the heat down to your pre-calibrated "15 PSI Hold" setting.
2.  Start your recipe's timer *only* after the weight has established a steady, rhythmic rock.
3.  Monitor periodically to ensure the wobble remains constant.

## 4. Depressurization
1.  When the timer finishes, turn the heat completely off.
2.  **Never force-cool the cooker.** Do not remove the weight early. Let it sit until the safety valve drops naturally (usually 45 to 60 minutes).
