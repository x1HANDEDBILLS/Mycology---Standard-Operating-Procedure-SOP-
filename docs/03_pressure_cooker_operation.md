# Pressure Cooker Operation

**Safety Warning:** Never let the pressure cooker run dry to avoid warping.

## Testing & Calibration
1.  Add 8 cups of water to the PC.
2.  Ensure rubber gasket is installed and lid is tight. Turn heat on high.
3.  Wait for the safety valve to pop up, locking the lid.
4.  **Venting:** Let steam blow out the valve for 10 minutes at 5 to 8 psi.
5.  **Pressurizing:** Place the 15-psi weight on the nipple. 
6.  **Balancing:** When the weight starts to wobble, turn the heat down to the minimum required to maintain a slow wobble. If it wobbles too fast, you are losing water. Wait 5 minutes between heat adjustments to allow the PC to stabilize.
