# 04 - Fruiting Tub Assembly

**Required Tool:** Step Bit. A normal drill bit will crack the 27 Qt Sterilite tub.
**Total Holes:** 10

## Long Sides (4 Holes Total)
1.  Measure **5 inches up** from the bottom.
2.  Measure **4.5 inches in** from the left corner. Drill a hole.
3.  Measure **4.5 inches in** from the right corner. Drill a hole.
4.  Repeat on the opposite long side.

## Short Sides (6 Holes Total)
**Bottom Holes:**
1.  Find the middle of the wall.
2.  Measure **5 inches up** from the bottom. Drill 1 hole.
3.  Repeat on the opposite short side.

**Top Holes:**
1.  Measure **1 inch down** from the middle of the handle latch. Make a mark.
2.  Measure **1 inch to the left** of that mark and drill a hole.
3.  Measure **1 inch to the right** of that mark and drill a hole.
4.  (You should have 2 holes that are 2 inches apart marked near the top).
5.  Repeat on the opposite short side.
