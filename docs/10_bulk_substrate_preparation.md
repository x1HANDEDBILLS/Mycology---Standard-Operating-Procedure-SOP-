# 10 - Bulk Substrate Preparation

*Using FloraFlex loose fill coco coir (already fluffy out of the bag).*

## Boiling
1.  Add **8 qts** of dry, loose coco to the 5-gal bucket.
2.  Fill your pressure cooker halfway with water. Place on burner set to high.
3.  Place the PC lid on top but **do not lock it**. Let water come to a strong roaring boil.
4.  Turn the burner off.

## Hydration & Pasteurization
1.  Quickly scoop out **16 cups** of the hot water and pour it onto the coco in the bucket.
2.  Place the lid on the bucket securely.
3.  Place the 5-gal bucket directly into the remaining hot water left inside the pressure cooker (this thermal mass allows the bucket to hold pasteurization temps for a longer period).
4.  Let it sit to pasteurize for at least 12 hours. (24 hours recommended to ensure it fully cools to room temperature).
