# Hardware Fabrication

## Building Lids
1.  **Layout:** Choose a layout with 2 filter patches, or 2 patches and 1 injection port.
2.  **Drilling:** Screw lid onto a jar. Drill 1/4-inch holes for filter patches and 7/16-inch holes for injection ports.
3.  **Spacing:** Keep holes evenly spaced. Ensure the center of the hole is at least 3/4 of an inch away from the very edge of the lid.
4.  **Cleaning:** Wash lids to remove plastic burrs. Use an Exacto blade if needed.
5.  **Assembly:** Push injection ports in first. Apply adhesive filter patches over the remaining holes.

## Building the Fruiting Tub (27 Qt)
*Use a step bit to avoid cracking the plastic.*
1.  **Long Sides:** Measure 5 inches up from the bottom and 4.5 inches from both the left and right corners. Drill 2 holes per long side.
2.  **Short Sides (Bottom):** Find the middle of the wall, measure 5 inches up, and drill 1 hole.
3.  **Short Sides (Top):** Measure 1 inch down from the middle of the handle. Measure 1 inch left and 1 inch right from that mark. Drill these 2 holes.
4.  **Total:** You should have 10 holes drilled.
