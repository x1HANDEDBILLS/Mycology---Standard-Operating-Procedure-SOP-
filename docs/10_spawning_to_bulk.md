# 10 - Spawning to Bulk

*PPE Recommended: Face mask and alcohol-sterilized gloves. Showering prior is highly recommended.*

## Tub Preparation
1.  Wipe out the inside of the 27qt tub and lid with alcohol. Let sit for a minute and dry.
2.  Dump **2 colonized grain jars** into the tub (Approx. a 1:3 ratio of grain to coco).

## Achieving Field Capacity (The Sponge Test)
1.  Open the 5-gal bucket of pasteurized coco. Grab a handful at a time.
2.  Slowly squeeze the coco in your hand to ring out excess water.
3.  **Target:** Water should just be dripping out.
    * *Too Wet:* A continuous stream of water pours out.
    * *Too Dry:* Very little water comes out, or requires maximum strength to get drops.
4.  Aim for the moisture of a kitchen sponge after a quick squeeze.

## Mixing
1.  Add squeezed coco handfuls into the tub until it fills the bottom 3 to 4 inches thick. Leave some coco in the bucket for the top layer.
2.  Mix the coco and grain thoroughly. Ensure it is leveled out evenly in the tub.
3.  Add a thin layer of coco across the top—just enough to cover all visible grains.
4.  Spray the surface with a fine mist of water for about 20 seconds. 
5.  Place the lid on the tub.
