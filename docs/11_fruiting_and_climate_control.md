# 11 - Fruiting and Climate Control

## Environment
* **Location:** A darkish area (like a closet) is perfect. 
* **Lighting:** Mushrooms do not need light to grow, but a small LED placed above them gives them a direction to grow toward.
* **Temperature:** Keep between **70°F to 80°F** (72°F to 74°F is ideal).
* **Airflow:** Ensure no fans or air currents are directly blowing on the tubs, as this causes them to dry out too quickly.

## Maintenance
1.  The tub is immediately in fruiting conditions upon creation.
2.  Contamination risk is low at this stage, but do not open the tub more than necessary.
3.  Only open the lid to mist the coco **twice a day**. 
4.  No additional air exchange is needed; the drilled holes provide the correct balance.
5.  Expect it to take 2 weeks to fully colonize in the tub. Keep misting 2 times daily until pins form.
