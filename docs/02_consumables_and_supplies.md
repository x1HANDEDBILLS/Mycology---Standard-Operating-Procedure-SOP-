# 02 - Consumables and Supplies

These are the recurring materials you will need to restock over time.

## Substrate & Grain
* **Popcorn:** Yellow popping corn, 4 lbs (~5$ for two 2lb bags)
* **Coco Coir:** Loose fill bag preferred (e.g., FloraFlex 50L) (~25$)
* **Corn Syrup:** Light corn syrup with real vanilla (~3$)

## Agar Ingredients
* **Agar Powder:** (~12$)
* **Extra Light Malt Extract (LME):** (~12$)
* **Food Coloring:** Optional (~5$)

## Lab Supplies
* **Filter Patches:** Adherable 0.3 micron filters (~10$ - 20$)
* **Injection Ports:** Optional, for liquid culture or syringes (~7$)
* **Portion Cups:** 3.25oz for agar pours (~8$)
* **Cheese Cloth:** Optional, for straining agar (~6$)
* **Scalpel Blades:** Sterile replacements
* **70% Isopropyl Alcohol:** (~3$)
* **Gloves:** Nitrile or latex (~12$)
* **Arm Sleeves:** Optional (~7$)
* **Paper Towels**
